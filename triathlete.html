About Esau

I have been a swimmer since the age of four. Moving to El Paso gave me the opportunity to join my high school swimming team. When I was not competing with the swim team, soccer served as my distraction. After proving to my swimming coach I could run a 5k before practice he introduced me to triathlons. I have been part of the triathlon world since 2007 having completed sprint, Olympic and Half Ironman distances. Now it’s time to take it to the next level!
